    • IEEE International Conference on Signal Processing and Communication(ICSC), Noida, 2016

      • -“Analysis of Neural Electrical Activities During Elicitation of Human Emotion based on EEG.”


    • This work aims to analyze the neural electrical activity inside the brain during elicitation of human emotional states using Electroencephalographic (EEG) signal. Such analysis offers to understand the human emotional process in great details .To do so, firstly, the emotional states are categorized in dimensional space based on valance-arousal scores into four classes: HVHA, HVLA, LVHA, LVLA. The results show that, for higher valance, the brain active regions in cortical level is same but the Brodmann areas are different. Such results suggests that there is different sets of neural active regions with different current densities for different classes of emotional states.
